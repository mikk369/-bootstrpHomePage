Secret

Team Secret is a European team that was first established during the post-TI4 shuffle. The original team drew upon players from former Natus Vincere, Fnatic and Alliance squads. The team has since undergone a number of reincarnations, but uniformly under the captaincy of Clement 'Puppey' Ivanov (from Estonia). Since its formation Team Secret have been one of the most successful brands in Dota 2, not only in Europe but on the international stage as well.
